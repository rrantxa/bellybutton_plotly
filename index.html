ABOUT THE PROJECT

The purpose of this project was to analyze the types of bacteria cultures found on a series of human subject's belly buttons. This way, the researchers involved in the project could take the information and determine if the bacteria cultures present could serve for the creation of vegan meat. You can take a look at the data below and see the results for yourself.
